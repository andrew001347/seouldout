Our Story

Seoul’dOut began with one idea: bring the taste and energy of real Korean barbecue to Los Angeles.

We grew up around sizzling grills, shared plates, and late night meals with friends. Now we serve that same spirit with quality meats, fresh banchan, and a lively atmosphere that feels like home.

Welcome to Seoul’dOut. Come hungry and leave happy.
